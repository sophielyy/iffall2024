Characters

  • Steve

    Steve is the main protagonist to the popular sandbox game, Minecraft. Despite being one of the most iconic characters in video game culture, very little is known about him.

  • Creeper

    Creepers are a hostile mob in the video game Minecraft, They are hostile mobs that would sneak up to the player, hiss, and explode.

  • Zombie

    Zombies are undead hostile mobs and one of the central antagonists in the game. Zombies can spawn from monster spawners inside dungeons.

  • Chicken

    The chicken is a passive mob found in grass biomes and the main source of raw chicken, feathers and eggs. A chicken can be attached to a lead.

  • Ender Dragon

    The Ender Dragon is a draconic creature and the first boss in Minecraft franchise. She is a gigantic, black dragon homed in The End. The Ender Dragon is able to destroy any blocks it comes into contact with, except obsidian, end stone, bedrock, command blocks and barriers..
